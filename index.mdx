Onyx is a feature-rich, self-hostable AI chat platform that connects to your organization's knowledge. It runs on any LLM — cloud or local — and includes Agents, RAG, Deep Research, 40+ connectors, MCP support, and enterprise security built in.

## What Onyx can do

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Chat with any LLM" icon="message-bot">
    Works with OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Ollama, vLLM, and any LiteLLM-compatible provider.
  </Card>
  <Card title="RAG over your data" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Hybrid search plus knowledge graph retrieval across millions of indexed documents.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom Agents" icon="robot">
    Build AI Agents with custom instructions, knowledge bases, and tool access.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deep Research" icon="microscope">
    Multi-step agentic research that synthesizes answers from across your knowledge base.
  </Card>
  <Card title="MCP & Actions" icon="bolt">
    Give agents the ability to act on external systems via Model Context Protocol.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enterprise Security" icon="shield-halved">
    SSO (OIDC/SAML/OAuth2), RBAC, document-level permissions, and encrypted credentials.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy Onyx">
    Run one command to spin up the full stack with Docker Compose. See the [Quick Start guide](/quickstart).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your knowledge sources">
    Add connectors to Slack, Confluence, Google Drive, GitHub, and more from the Admin panel. See [Connectors](/features/connectors).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your LLM">
    Point Onyx at your preferred LLM provider — cloud or self-hosted. See [LLM Providers](/configuration/llm-providers).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Open the chat interface, select an assistant or create your own Agent, and start asking questions across your connected knowledge.
  </Step>
</Steps>
